BIO

My birth name is Daniel Kwesi Mensah but my artist identity is Scrapa. I spent most of my childhood In Lagos, Nigeria but currently reside In Accra, Ghana. I was brought up in a lively entertaining home. I love to visit new places, explore, entertainment and have fun. I am an art-driven individual who is a graffiti artist, musician, songwriter, model, actor, and MC. To answer the question of who I am, I’d say I’m an embodiment of art.
